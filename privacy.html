Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection

HatchTracker accesses message history in channels where it is invoked solely for the purpose of counting specific keywords (Egg names). No message content is stored, archived, or shared.

2. Data Storage

The bot stores configuration data (Channel IDs, Timezone settings) required for its functionality. This data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties.

3. Contact

For any privacy concerns, please contact the developer at: jul.auvinet@gmail.com or via the Support Discord.
